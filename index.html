Etch-a-Sketch

Instructions for Use

Hover over the grid to colour in the Etch-a-Sketch. Select a colour by clicking on the rectangle inside the button. To fill in the Etch-a-Sketch with random colours, tick the "Random Colour" box. To darken the colours in the Etch-a-Sketch, tick the "Darken" box. To clear the Etch-a-Sketch, click the "Clear" button. To create a new grid, click "Create New Grid" and specify the dimension of the new grid.
